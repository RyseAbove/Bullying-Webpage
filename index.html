What is bullying?

"Bullying is an ongoing and deliberate misuse of power in relationships through repeated verbal, physical and/or social behavior that intends to cause physical, social and/or psychological harm. It can involve an individual or a group misusing their power, or perceived power, over one or more persons who feel unable to stop it from happening." This is the definition of bullying written by the National Centre of Bullying.

There are many types of bullying but the most prominent are in-person and online (a.k.a. "cyber-bullying"). There are many different reasons and effects of bullying.
