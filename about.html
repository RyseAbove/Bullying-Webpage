About How to Help

First, we have to explain what bullying is. Bullying is when a person or group of people abuse their power by physically or mentally attacking someone. This can happen in many ways and places.

If you are looking to do some research on this topic, you can go to sites like stopbullying.gov and ncab.org.au for more information. They are some other good and trustworthy sources of information.

Next, we have to define what cyber-bullying is. Cyber-bullying is defined as "the use of electronic communication to bully a person, typically by sending messages of an intimidating or threatening nature". While there are other things that can happen through cyber-bullying, this is a good definition.
